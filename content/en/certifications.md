## Why the certifications matter

Security, regulatory compliance and transparency are critical in organisations where data handling, operations and supplier relationships are regularly reviewed. For us, compliance is not only about meeting requirements, but about building trust through a way of working that is clear, traceable and predictable.

That is why we continue to invest in security, governance and operational processes. By keeping key certifications and supporting documentation readily available, we reduce the time and manual effort often required during procurements, customer reviews and audits.

## About Safespring

Safespring is a Swedish-owned Cloud Service Provider offering secure and scalable cloud infrastructure, including a managed Kubernetes platform for containerised applications. We specialise in serving organisations with high demands on security, compliance and operational reliability.

We operate with a strong focus on:

- security and data protection
- reliability and availability
- regulatory compliance, including GDPR
- data sovereignty and control
- continuous improvement

Our services are designed for regulated and security-conscious environments where resilience, scalability and control matter. With data hosted in Sweden and within the EU, we provide strong data sovereignty and an operating model aligned with both GDPR and internationally recognised standards.

## Our services

### Safespring Compute

Safespring Compute is an Infrastructure-as-a-Service offering built on OpenStack. It provides robust server capacity from secure data centers and is available as both a public cloud service and a dedicated private environment. Customers can create, start and stop virtual machines through a self-service portal or a standards-based API.

### Safespring Storage

Safespring Storage is a scalable object storage service built on Ceph with an S3-compatible interface. It is available both as a public cloud service and as a private storage solution for organisations that require dedicated clusters, high availability and strong performance for large volumes of unstructured data.

### Safespring Backup

Safespring Backup is an enterprise-grade cloud backup service for physical and virtual servers based on IBM Spectrum Protect. The solution is delivered from our nodes in Stockholm but can be used for data protection from any location.

### Safespring Kubernetes Platform

Safespring Kubernetes Platform is a managed Kubernetes service that provides a fully operated control plane. This allows customers to focus on applications and delivery instead of the underlying platform. The service is available as both a public and a private solution and is built for security, scalability and ease of operation.

## Our customers

We support organisations in regulated environments, including public sector bodies, government agencies, healthcare providers and private companies with high requirements for data protection and compliance. For many of these customers, data sovereignty is a key concern, and our platform is designed to provide clear control over where data is stored and processed.

## Our certifications

Two certifications are especially important in how we demonstrate structure, security and continuous improvement across the business.

ISO 27001 is the internationally recognised standard for information security management systems. It shows that we work in a structured way to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information through policies, risk management, controls and continuous improvement.

FR2000 complements this by combining requirements for quality, environmental management and occupational health and safety in one management framework. Together, the certifications provide a clearer picture of how Safespring works with both information security and operational governance.
