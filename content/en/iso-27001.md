ISO/IEC 27001:2023 is the internationally recognised standard for information security management systems. For reviews and audits, the key points are the certification scope, validity period and the fact that the certificate is issued by RISE Research Institutes of Sweden.

## Certification scope

Safespring's ISO certification covers sales, operations, development and maintenance of virtual and physical platform infrastructure for public and private cloud services. The certification applies in accordance with our Statement of Applicability dated 2026-03-23.

## What the certificate confirms

The formal certificate issued by RISE states that Safespring AB has an information security management system that fulfils the requirements of SS-EN ISO/IEC 27001:2023 for:

- sales
- operations
- development
- maintenance of virtual and physical platform infrastructure for public and private cloud services

This makes the certification directly relevant to how our cloud services are designed, delivered and followed up in practice.

## Why this matters in reviews and audits

ISO 27001 provides an established reference point for how Safespring governs information security. It helps customers, procurement teams and auditors review our security governance against a recognised standard with a clearly defined scope and validity period.

## How Safespring uses ISO 27001

For Safespring, ISO 27001 confirms that information security is an integrated part of the business rather than a one-time initiative. The certification supports our work with policies, risk management, controls, documentation and continuous improvement across the organisation.
