ISO/IEC 27001:2023 is the internationally recognised standard for information security management systems. The certification shows that Safespring works in a structured way to establish, implement, maintain and continually improve information security.
{{< /ingress >}}

In practice, the certification shows that Safespring works systematically to protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of information through policies, risk management, controls and continuous improvement.

## Certification scope

Safespring's ISO certification covers sales, operations, development and maintenance of virtual and physical platform infrastructure for public and private cloud services. The certification applies in accordance with our Statement of Applicability dated 2026-03-23.

## What the certificate confirms

The formal certificate issued by RISE states that Safespring AB has an information security management system that fulfils the requirements of SS-EN ISO/IEC 27001:2023 for:

- sales
- operations
- development
- maintenance of virtual and physical platform infrastructure for public and private cloud services

This makes the certification directly relevant to how our cloud services are designed, delivered and followed up in practice.

## What ISO 27001 means for Safespring

For Safespring, ISO 27001 confirms that information security is not a one-time effort but an integrated part of the business. The certification supports our work with governance, documentation, risk management and recurring review.

## What it means for our customers

For customers, the certification provides additional assurance that we work according to an established and internationally recognized standard. This is particularly relevant in organizations with high requirements for traceability, risk management and compliance.

## Why the certification matters in audits and customer reviews

ISO 27001 is a clear reference point for how we demonstrate our security governance in audits and customer reviews. When certificates, scope and supporting security information are clearly documented, the review process becomes easier for both customers and auditors.

## Continuous improvement

ISO 27001 is built around the idea that security work must be reviewed and improved over time. The certification therefore also supports our continued improvement, both internally and in dialogue with customers, partners and auditors. It also helps make customer audits and external reviews more efficient by clearly documenting responsibilities, controls and scope.
