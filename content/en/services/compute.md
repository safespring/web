## OpenStack-based IaaS in Sweden

{{< ingress >}}
Safespring Compute gives organizations a flexible infrastructure platform for virtual servers, automation, and scalable operations without hyperscaler lock-in.
{{< /ingress >}}

With Safespring, you can create, start, and stop virtual machines in just a few simple steps. It is easy to adapt to your needs thanks to our self-service and complete automation.

### Your data is GDPR-secure

We offer the highest level of security and availability with our data centers located within Sweden's borders. Safespring Compute is flexible, automated, and simplifies compliance with data storage laws and regulations. All data is stored on encrypted hard drives.

With Safespring Compute, you gain access to market-leading cloud technology directly from our secure and reliable data centers in Sweden. Your data stays within the country's borders, providing extra data security.

{{% accordion title="VPN services" %}}

With Safespring Compute, you can strengthen your organization with robust and flexible VPN options like WireGuard and ZeroTier. Our automated solution for WireGuard VPN servers provides a secure and efficient starting point for accessing private networks from any location, perfect for companies that require remote access and high data privacy. WireGuard offers a streamlined and easy-to-configure VPN architecture that ensures your company data is secure and accessible regardless of where your employees are.

For organizations seeking an even more scalable VPN solution, ZeroTier is an excellent alternative. With ZeroTier, you can easily create virtual networks with encrypted tunnels that enable secure and immediate communication between nodes globally. This platform is ideal for quick configuration and easy scalability without the need for traditional VPN hardware or complex installation processes.

At Safespring, we are proud to offer these advanced VPN capabilities integrated directly into our Compute platform. With just a few clicks, you can configure and deploy a complete VPN solution that supports both WireGuard and ZeroTier, ensuring that your organization always has access to secure and flexible network options.

[Read more in our documentation](https://docs.safespring.com/compute/vpn/).

## Central block storage

{{< ingress >}}
Our centralized block storage offers secure and flexible storage capacity that is separate from the nodes where your instances run.
{{< /ingress >}}

This enables high availability and reliability. Through a distributed Ceph cluster, we can guarantee optimal performance and scalability for your specific needs. Our block storage options come in two variants, adapted for different use cases: FAST and LARGE.

{{< icon-block-horisontal color="#195F8C" icon="fas fa-rocket" text="FAST" description="Our FAST storage is optimized for performance and designed for applications that require low latency and high IOPS (Input/Output Operations Per Second). It is ideal for databases, virtual machines, and other performance-critical applications that require fast response times and reliable availability." >}}
{{< icon-block-horisontal color="#195F8C" icon="fas fa-hdd" text="LARGE" description="LARGE storage is designed to offer cost-effective block storage with high capacity. It is the perfect choice for applications where storage capacity is prioritized over performance, such as archiving, backups, and handling large datasets where cost per gigabyte is a decisive factor." >}}

## OpenStack and Kubernetes
OpenStack and Kubernetes make it easy to automate and scale your application setup. No matter where you choose to run your applications, Safespring can offer a unified and smooth solution.

Although our platform is based on open source and Linux, we also support Windows on our virtual machines. Safespring works with CloudBase to offer ready-to-use Windows images, so you can get started quickly.
