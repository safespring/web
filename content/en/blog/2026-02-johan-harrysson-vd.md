The Swedish cloud provider Safespring announces today that it is strengthening its management team. Johan Harrysson has been recruited as the new Chief Executive Officer (CEO) in a strategic move to lead the company forward following strong growth over the past five years.

Johan Harrysson, with experience as CEO of fast-growing companies in regulated industries, is a familiar name to the company, as he previously held the role of CCO (Chief Commercial Officer) at Safespring.

{{< quote "Johan Harrysson, CEO" >}}
– It’s fantastic to be back at Safespring and working with our strong team. Safespring today delivers a superb, resilient and compliant cloud service for running critical applications on Safespring’s public and private cloud platforms, as well as in federated environments under European jurisdiction.
{{< /quote >}}

## Safespring sharpens its focus on regulated industries and hybrid cloud
In conjunction with the CEO transition, Safespring’s founder and former CEO, Fredric Wallsten, will take over as Chief Commercial Officer (CCO). Going forward, his mission will be to wholeheartedly develop collaboration with the company’s strategic customers in life science, healthcare, research, and other operations where security, regulatory compliance, and trust are business-critical.

{{< quote "Fredric Wallsten, CCO" >}}
– We continue at full speed to support our customers who are building mission-critical solutions in hybrid cloud. We help them combine Safespring’s private and public cloud platforms with sovereign data control, high compute capacity, and secure storage of very large volumes of data – while meeting strict requirements for regulatory compliance and data protection, says Fredric Wallsten, CCO at Safespring.
{{< /quote >}}

Through the reorganization, Safespring ensures that management has the right structure to take the next growth step and meet strong market demand. 
 
### About Safespring 
Safespring is a Swedish cloud platform that complies with laws and regulations within the EU. With a focus on open standards and digital sovereignty, Safespring offers services such as compute, large-scale storage, and container services. With data centers in Sweden and Norway, Safespring is a proven and secure platform for government agencies, data-driven companies, and the academic sector.
